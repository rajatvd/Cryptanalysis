Welcome!

This IPython notebook contains the cryptanalysis of the Caeser and Vigenere ciphers. The analysis includes methods of frequency analysis for the Caeser cipher with detailed bar graphs to understand the method better. It also uses the Kasiski examination to crack the Vigenere cipher.
